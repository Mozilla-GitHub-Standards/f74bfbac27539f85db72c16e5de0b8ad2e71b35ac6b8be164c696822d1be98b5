So, what’s Linkdrop?

Linkdrop is a browser extension that allows you to share links in a fast and fun way. Share links from within the browser, from any webpage, using the same services you already know and love. Linkdrop is made by Mozilla Messaging.
“A browser extension for sharing” Check out the video to see how it works

Share with your favourite services...

Twitter With one click Linkdrop automatically shortens URLs using bit.ly so that you don't have to. Linkdrop then inserts the shortened URL into your tweet, making the whole process seamless.
Facebook Linkdrop posts the URL, complete with screenshot and description right to your Facebook wall, instantly sharing the link with all your friends on Facebook.
Gmail Linkdrop automatically inserts the URL into the body of your email - simply type in your recipients Email addresses and hit send.

Once you've installed Linkdrop sharing is as easy as...

1 Hit the share icon in the toolbar
2 Choose your service in the menu
3 Express yourself and hit share!

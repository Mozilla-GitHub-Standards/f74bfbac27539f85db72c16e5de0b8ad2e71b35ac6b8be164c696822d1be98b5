So, what’s Linkdrop?

Linkdrop is your conversation starter. Linkdrop is a browser extension that allows you to easily share, track and archive links in a fast and fun way. Share links from within the browser, from any webpage, using the same services you already know and love. Linkdrop is a product of Mozilla Messaging.
